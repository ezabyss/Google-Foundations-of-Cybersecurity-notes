## ğŸ­ Threat Actors

A **threat actor** is *any* person or group that poses a security risk.

### External Threat  
Someone *outside* the organization attempting to gain unauthorized access.

### Internal Threat  
A threat originating from:

- employees  
- ex-employees  
- contractors  
- partners  

Internal threats can be:

- **Accidental** (like clicking a malicious email)
- **Intentional** (like abusing systems or accessing data without permission)

Security professionals help organizations **reduce, prevent, and respond** to these threats.
