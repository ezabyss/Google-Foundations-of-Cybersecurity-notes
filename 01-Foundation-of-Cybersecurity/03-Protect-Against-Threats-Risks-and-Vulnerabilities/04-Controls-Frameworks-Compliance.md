## ğŸ” What Are Security Controls?

**Controls** = safeguards that reduce specific security risks.

Examples:

- Access controls  
- MFA  
- Encryption  
- Audit logs  
- Training requirements  

Controls enforce framework rules and help organizations meet compliance obligations.

## ğŸ§± What Are Security Frameworks?

**Frameworks** = structured guidelines for managing security programs.

They help organizations:

- Build plans  
- Identify risks  
- Protect data & privacy  
- Align with regulations  

### **The four core components of every framework:**
1. **Identify & document security goals**  
2. **Set guidelines** to achieve the goals  
3. **Implement strong processes**  
4. **Monitor & communicate results**

Framework example goals:  
- GDPR compliance  
- PCI DSS security requirements  
- HIPAA privacy protection
