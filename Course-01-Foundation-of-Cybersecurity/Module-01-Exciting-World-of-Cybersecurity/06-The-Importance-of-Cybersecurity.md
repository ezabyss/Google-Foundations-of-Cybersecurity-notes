## ðŸ§© Understanding PII and SPII

### **PII (Personally Identifiable Information)**  
Data that can identify a specific person, such as:

- Full name  
- Date of birth  
- Physical address  
- Phone number  
- Email address  
- IP address  

### **SPII (Sensitive Personally Identifiable Information)**  
A stricter category that requires higher protection, including:

- Social security numbers  
- Medical records  
- Financial information  
- Biometric data (fingerprints, facial recognition)  

**SPII leaks are far more dangerous** and often lead to severe consequences.
