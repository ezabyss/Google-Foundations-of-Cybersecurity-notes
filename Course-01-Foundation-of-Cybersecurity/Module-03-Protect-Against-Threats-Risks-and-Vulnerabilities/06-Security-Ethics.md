# üîí 4. Ethical Principle #1 ‚Äî Confidentiality

Confidentiality = protecting sensitive data and limiting access to authorized individuals only.

### Examples of ethical responsibilities:
- Keep PII and proprietary information secure  
- Do not disclose data outside proper channels  
- Do not provide unauthorized system access ‚Äî even to friends  
- Never use privileged access for personal benefit  

### Violations lead to:
- Disciplinary actions  
- Loss of job or reputation  
- Legal consequences  
- Organizational damage  

**Rule of thumb:**  
If it‚Äôs not properly documented or authorized, *don‚Äôt do it*.
