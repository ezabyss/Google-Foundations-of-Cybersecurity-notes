# üîê 1. What Is the Responsibility When Collecting PII?

When an organization collects PII on behalf of another person, analysts and engineers must:

- Handle it **deliberately and responsibly**  
- Know **where the data is stored** at all times  
- Ensure the storage location is secure  
- Understand any **legal or regulatory obligations** attached to that data  

PII can trigger requirements under laws such as:

- GDPR  
- HIPAA  
- CCPA  
- PCI DSS  
- FedRAMP  
- And many others depending on the jurisdiction

# üåç 3. Complexity of PII Regulations

PII laws are not universal. They vary widely by:

- Country  
- State  
- Region  
- Sector  

This makes PII one of the most complicated areas of compliance.

Examples:

- GDPR governs EU residents' data  
- HIPAA governs U.S. healthcare data  
- CCPA protects California residents  
- PCI DSS governs payment card processing  

Each has different requirements and penalties.
