## ‚úÖ What Is Compliance?
**Compliance** is the process of:

- Following **external regulations**

In simple terms:

## ‚öñÔ∏è Regulations vs Policies

## üß© Introducing the NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF)

The **NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF)** is:
- A **voluntary** framework
- Based in the United States
- Designed to manage cybersecurity risk

It helps organizations protect one of their most valuable assets:
> **Information**

---

## üß± Components of the NIST CSF
The CSF has **three main components**:
1. **Core**
2. **Tiers**
3. **Profiles**

Each answers a different security question.

---

# 1Ô∏è‚É£ CSF Core ‚Äî *What Security Does*

## üìå Definition
The **CSF Core** defines desired cybersecurity outcomes.  
It acts as a **security checklist**.

---

## üîÅ CSF Core Functions (Updated)
The Core consists of **six functions**:

1. **Identify**
2. **Protect**
3. **Detect**
4. **Respond**
5. **Recover**
6. **Govern** *(added in 2024)*

---

## üîç Identify
Focus:
- Asset management
- Risk assessment
- Governance awareness

üìå *Know what you have and what could go wrong.*

### üß† SOC Scenario
SOC receives an alert but cannot determine severity.

**Root cause:**  
Asset not inventoried.

> Unknown assets = unknown risk.

---

## üõ°Ô∏è Protect
Focus:
- Access control
- Training
- Safeguards

üìå *Prevent incidents before they occur.*

### üß† SOC Scenario

- MFA blocks access
- Damage prevented

---

## üëÄ Detect
Focus:
- Monitoring
- Logging
- Alerts

üìå *Find incidents quickly.*

### üß† SOC Scenario
SOC detects abnormal login attempts at midnight.

**Action:**  
Alert escalated immediately.

---

## üö® Respond
Focus:
- Incident response
- Containment
- Communication

üìå *Limit the impact.*

### üß† SOC Scenario
Confirmed breach detected.

SOC actions:
1. Isolate systems
2. Notify stakeholders
3. Preserve evidence

---

## üîÑ Recover
Focus:
- Restoring systems
- Improving resilience

üìå *Return to normal operations.*

### üß† SOC Scenario

---

## üèõÔ∏è Govern (New Function ‚Äì 2024)
Focus:
- Leadership
- Policy decisions
- Risk oversight

üìå *Ensures accountability and direction.*

### üß† SOC Scenario

| Tier | Name | Meaning |
|----|----|----|
