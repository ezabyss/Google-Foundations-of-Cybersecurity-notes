## üåç Why Attack Surface Analysis Comes First

Organizations face **many vulnerabilities**, but resources are limited.

To stay ahead of threats, security teams begin by understanding the **environment around their operations**. This starts with analyzing the **attack surface**.

An **attack surface** is the **sum of all possible points** where a threat actor could exploit a vulnerability.

> üß† The larger the attack surface, the harder it is to defend.

## üõ°Ô∏è Security Hardening (Physical)

* Strengthening systems
* Reducing vulnerabilities

In simple terms:

> üîí Fewer entry points = easier protection

* Access controls
* Device usage rules
* Employee awareness training
