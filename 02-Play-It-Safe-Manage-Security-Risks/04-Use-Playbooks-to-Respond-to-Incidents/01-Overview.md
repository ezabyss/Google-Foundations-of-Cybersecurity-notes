A **playbook** is a documented guide that outlines:
- Step-by-step actions
- Roles and responsibilities
- Procedures to follow during a security event

Playbooks ensure that security teams:
- Respond consistently
- Reduce mistakes under pressure
- Act quickly during incidents

**Incident response** is the organized approach an organization takes to:

- Limiting impact
- Protecting assets
- Maintaining trust and operations
