## 2Ô∏è‚É£ What Is a CSIRT?

A **Computer Security Incident Response Team (CSIRT)** is a specialized group of security professionals trained in:

- Incident detection
- Incident analysis
- Incident containment and recovery
- Post-incident improvement

### üéØ Primary Goals of a CSIRT

- Manage incidents **effectively and efficiently**
- Provide services and resources for **response and recovery**
- Help **prevent future incidents** through lessons learned

üìå Security is a **shared responsibility**, so CSIRTs work cross-functionally with other departments.

---

## 3Ô∏è‚É£ Cross-Functional Collaboration (Why It Matters)

CSIRTs frequently collaborate with **non-security teams**, depending on the incident.

### Examples:

- **Legal team**
  - Required when incidents involve:
    - Personally identifiable information (PII)
    - Financial records
    - Regulatory obligations

- **Public relations (PR) team**
  - Some regulations require **public disclosure** within a specific timeframe
  - CSIRTs and PR must coordinate messaging to avoid:
    - Legal risk
    - Reputational damage
    - Misinformation

üìå Incident response is as much about **communication and compliance** as it is about technology.

### üß† Security Analyst

The **front line** of incident response.

Responsibilities:
- Investigate security alerts
- Determine whether an alert is an actual incident
- Assign a **criticality rating**
- Remediate low-impact incidents when possible

üìå Not all incidents require escalation.

---

### üõ†Ô∏è Technical Lead (Ops Lead)

Responsibilities:
- Provide technical leadership
- Guide incidents through the full lifecycle
- Make advanced response decisions
- Coordinate technical remediation

üìå This role may also be called:
- Ops Lead
- Incident Lead

---

### üß≠ Incident Coordinator

The **organizational backbone** of incident response.

Responsibilities:
- Track incident progress

- Ensure response procedures are followed
- Provide regular status updates to stakeholders

üìå This role ensures **nothing falls through the cracks**.

## 5Ô∏è‚É£ CSIRT Variations Across Organizations

Not all organizations use the same terminology or structure.

A CSIRT may also be called:
- **Incident Handling Team (IHT)**
- **Security Incident Response Team (SIRT)**

Some teams:
- Focus only on **crisis management**
- Are integrated with a **Security Operations Center (SOC)**
- Specialize in certain incident types

Regardless of structure or naming, the **mission remains the same**:
> Effective incident management and response.
