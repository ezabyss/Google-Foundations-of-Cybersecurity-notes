## ğŸ§‘â€ğŸ’¼ Workplace Example: Applying the NIST CSF

### Scenario

A high-risk alert indicates that a workstation has been compromised.

### Step-by-Step Using the CSF

* **Govern**
  Security policies and risk management processes define how incidents are handled and who is accountable.

* **Identify**
  The compromised workstation is identified, and an unknown device is discovered.

* **Protect**
  The unknown device is blocked remotely to prevent further damage.

* **Detect**
  Security tools are used to look for additional threat actor activity and identify the device.

* **Respond**
  The incident is investigated to determine:

  * Who connected the device
  * How the threat occurred
  * What systems were affected
  * Where the attack originated

* **Recover**
  Affected files are restored, and damage to the workstation is corrected.
